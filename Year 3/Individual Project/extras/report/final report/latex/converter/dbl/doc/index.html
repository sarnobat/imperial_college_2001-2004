DocBook Conformance

DB2LaTeX was developed with DocBook: The Definitive Guide 1st edition in mind. In time, it will be refined for the 2nd edition.

Feel free to submit non-conformance situations as bugs. You could report bugs via the SourceForge tracker or discuss them on one of the mailing lists.
