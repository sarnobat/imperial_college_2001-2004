Name

latex.apply.title.templates.admonitions — Whether to apply templates for admonition titles.

Synopsis

<xsl:param name="latex.apply.title.templates.admonitions">1</xsl:param>

Controls whether admonition titles will be generated by applying templates or by conversion to string values. When enabled, templates will be applied.
