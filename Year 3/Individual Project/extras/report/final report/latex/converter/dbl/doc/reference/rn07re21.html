Name

bridgehead — A free-floating heading.

Synopsis

<xsl:template match="bridgehead"/>

Renders un-numbered section headings. For renderas values of sect1, sect2, and sect3, LaTeX “star” commands (such as \section*) are used. Otherwise, a bold heading is put on a line of its own.
